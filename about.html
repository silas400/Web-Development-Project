About Leslie

Leslie is an illustrator and graphic designer with an immense passion with art and technology. She is currently located in the heart of Los Angeles earning her IxD degree at SMC.

Her skills range from Character Design and Storyboard to UX Design and Web Development. She always strives to improve the quality of her craft and pushes herself to think outside the box.
